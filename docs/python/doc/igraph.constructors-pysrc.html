137        - C{---->} is the opposite of C{<----} 
138        - C{<--->} creates a mutual directed edge pair between 
139          the two vertices 
140

142      the graph will be undirected. Otherwise it will be directed. 
143      Vertex names used in the formula will be assigned to the

145
